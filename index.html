Prime

Creators
Prime is a range of sports drinks, drink mixes, and energy drinks created and marketed by Prime Hydration, LLC. The drink was released on 4th of January 2022 by the Youtubers KSI and Logan Paul. It was followed by a social media hype associated with these social media personalities, who have tens of millions of followers combined.

Products
The Prime labelling is used on sports drinks, energy drinks, and drink mixes. The sports drinks, described by the manufacturer as "hydration drinks", are made up of 10 percent coconut water and contain electrolytes, B vitamins and BCAAs. The manufacturers state that this version of the product has zero added sugar or caffeine and has around 20 calories per bottle. It is sweetened with acesulfame potassium and sucralose, like many other sugar-free drinks.
